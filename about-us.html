Our Work Accordion

What can I find at Garden World?

Garden World offers an unmatched selection of plant material grown by premier northwest growers. We stock over 15,000 plants in many different varieties

Can I purchase plants from Garden World?

Yes! We are a retail garden center. Our 10 acre nursery and online shopping inventory are open to everyone.

What do #1, #5 mean on your plants?

In the nursery industry, plastic containers are labeled #1, #2, #5, etc. The number means approximately 'gallon'.
